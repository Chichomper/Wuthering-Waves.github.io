PLOT/OVERVIEW

Wuthering Waves is a story-rich open-world action RPG with a high degree of freedom. You wake from your slumber as Rover, joined by a vibrant cast of Resonators on a journey to reclaim your lost memories and change the world.



Kuro Games

From the creators of Punishing Gray Raven

Kuro Games brings out their latest project, a game called Wuthering Waves Wuthering Waves is an Open World RPG set on the world of Solaris III Wuthering Waves also known as WuWa by Fans is the latest project of Kuro Games



Solaris-3

Solaris 3 is the Planet that the game takes place on, inhabited by creatures of varying degree, like rock like golems to giant lizards Solaris-3 is also home to several regions, like Huanglong, The Black Shores, and Rinascita
