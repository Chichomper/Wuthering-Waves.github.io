About Huanglong

Huanglong is comprised of seven cities, with one being its capital and with each city having its own Sentinel.[1] The Mountains of Spirit running from north to south divides Huanglong into Inner Huanglong and Outer Huanglong. [2] The capital and the other five cities are in Inner Huanglong, while Jinzhou is in Outer Huanglong. Cities
