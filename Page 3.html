Who are they?

Kuro Games was founded in the year 2014, in Zhuhai China and are still around to this day, known for their Games Punishing Gray Raven, and what is being covered here, Wuthering Waves.

Punishing Gray Raven

What is it?

Punishing Gray Raven, which was Kuro Games' first project, it is a free-to-play action role-playing game developed and published by Kuro Games. It was first released in China on December 5, 2019, and a year later on December 4, 2020, in Japan. The game was released globally on July 16, 2021.
